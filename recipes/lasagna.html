Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna for Garfield.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna until just slightly before al dente, then drain
  2. Spread one layer of noodles on bottom of pan
  3. Add cooked ground beef, tomato sauce, cheese, onion, and garlic
  4. Cover with another layer of noodles
  5. Repeat step 3 until pan is full and cover with a final layer of noodles
  6. Sprinkle more cheese on top
  7. Bake until cheese is melted and starting to darken in some spots
  8. Enjoy!
